            Transaction History

            In your transaction history, you'll find a comprehensive record of your financial activities. It
            details the timing and specifics of shares bought or sold, ensuring complete transparency and
            accuracy. Each entry includes the following essential information:

            1. Timing: The precise date and time of each transaction are recorded, allowing you to track
            the chronological order of your financial activities.

            2. Share Symbol: The unique symbol or ticker representing the shares involved in the
            transaction, ensuring clarity and easy identification.

            3. Shares Buy or Sell: Clearly specifies whether the transaction involved buying or selling
            shares. This distinction is crucial for monitoring your investment strategy.

            4. Amount of Transaction: Provides the monetary value of the transaction, making it easy to
            assess the financial impact of each trade.
